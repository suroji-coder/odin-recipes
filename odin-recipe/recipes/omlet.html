Omelet recipe

Description

This is a simple omelet recipe cooked with oil. It is quick and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Oil
  • Onion
  • Salt

Steps

  1. cut the onion into small pieces
  2. Beat the eggs in a bowl and mix with the onion
  3. heat the oil
  4. add the mixture and cook for 2-3 min
