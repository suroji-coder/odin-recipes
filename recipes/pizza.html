Pizza

Description

This is a simple homemade pizza recipe. It is easy to prepare and tastes delicious.

Ingredients

  • pizza dough
  • tomato sauce
  • cheese
  • vegetables

Steps

  1. Prepare the pizza dough.
  2. Spread tomato sauce on the dough.
  3. Add cheese and vegetables on top.
  4. Bake in the oven for 15–20 minutes.
